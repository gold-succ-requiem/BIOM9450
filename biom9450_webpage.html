Every year, the Australian Health Informatics Association (AHIA) runs the Australian Health Informatics Conference (AHICon). AHICon workshops bring together professionals and academics in medicine and information science to develop more robust healthcare systems.

AHICon 2019 will be held on 16-17 December 2019 in Sydney. This year's conference will focus on current developments in ICD-10-AM and HL7, standards for disease coding and health messaging undergoing change.

Our Focus

This year's focus will be on ICD-10-AM and HL7 standards for disease coding and health messaging, and will be the topic of two plenary seminars. Standards which the Australian healthcare system relies on are currently changing. Read more about our focus HERE.
